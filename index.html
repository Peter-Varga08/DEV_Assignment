Welcome to the developer assignment

Description:

This is a simple exercise on arithmetics. You will be asked to calculate the sum of two random numbers. After clicking on the start button, you will have to submit an answer for which you are going to receive a feedback, about whether it was correct or not.

You may also set some parameters below for the exercise.

If you fail to give the correct answer, you may try again. Otherwise a new exercise will be generated on the next attempt.
